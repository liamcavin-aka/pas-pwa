Personal Agency Scorecard (PAS)

Measure your day. Strengthen your agency. Improve your life—one decision at a time.

PAS is a personal instrument designed to help you see your patterns clearly, align your daily behavior with your long-term goals, and strengthen your ability to direct your own life intentionally.

Open the PAS App Install on Phone

How it works

  • Answer 15 honest yes/no questions across five pillars
  • Each pillar generates a color score: Green, Yellow, Orange, or Red
  • You receive a constructive message and one action step for tomorrow
  • Track your weekly trends and export your progress as a personal PDF report
PAS is private. Your data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

Founder Note

PAS was created to solve a personal problem: how to know, with honesty, whether each day strengthened or weakened your future.

Most people live without clear feedback. Days pass. Weeks pass. Years pass. Without measurement, there is no clarity. Without clarity, there is no control.

PAS gives you that clarity. It shows you where you are aligned and where you are not. It gives you one step forward each day. Over time, those steps compound.

Agency is not given. It is practiced. PAS helps you practice it daily.

— PAS Founder

Install PAS on your phone

iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open the PAS app: Open the PAS App
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with arrow)
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add
Android (Chrome)
  1. Open the PAS app: Open the PAS App
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu
  3. Tap Install app
  4. Tap Install
After installing, PAS works offline and can be opened like a normal app.
